About Solace Property Rental

Solace Property Rental was created out of a hatred toward horric fees set my estate agents.

Solace allows you to create an account and advertise your property for rental.

By skipping out the middle men, as a property owner, you don't need to worry about paying extortionate fees to estate agencies. And as a tenant you don't have to worry about admin fees and can deal directly with the landlord.

We merely show what is available. The rest is up to you!
